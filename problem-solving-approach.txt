1. Understand the problem
- Clarify 
- Ask questions

2. Concrete Examples
- Understand inputs and outputs
- Understand edgecases

3. Break it down
- Steps/gameplan to code out and refer back to
- You can write pseudocode to do this or just write steps

4. Solve/simplify
- Either bruteforce or ignore parts of the question you can plug in later

5. Look back and refactor
